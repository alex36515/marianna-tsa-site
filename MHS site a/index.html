About TSA

Marianna Technology Student Association, or Marianna TSA, is a student based organization sponsored by Marianna High School that reaches out to MHS attendees who plan on pursuing a career in the STEM, or science, technology, engineering, and mathematics, fields. With over twenty youths participating in the program lead by Mrs. Vicki Garret, more and more teens are beginning to learn about their passion to become the pioneering engineers of tomorrow. From aspiring computer scientists to future architects, Marianna TSA is home to the next generation of innovators, who live by the national association's motto: "Learning to lead in a technical world."

Our Chapter

From the near four decade long path that National TSA has followed, our local Marianna TSA division has came about in the second half of the TSA organization's history. Seventeen years ago, the Technology Student Association was introduced to Marianna High School, and has since affected hundreds of teenager's lives by getting kids to explore there passion for technology through team membership and fun competition on the state and national level.
